Mara Corpa: Specialist in Piercing and Body Jewelry

In the world of piercing, few names stand out as much as Mara Corpa. Born in Madrid, her passion for the art of body jewelry started practically at birth. She grew up in a family deeply connected to the world of tattoos and piercings, which allowed her to experience this lifestyle closely from an early age. As a child, she had the opportunity to travel across Spain, attending conventions where she gained knowledge and experiences that would later shape her career.

Although she chose to train as a Higher Technician in Aesthetics, specializing in Micropigmentation during her youth, Mara always knew her true calling was piercing. Once she achieved the financial independence she needed, she fully pursued her dream. She attended numerous seminars and specialized courses, perfecting her technique and deepening her understanding of the best practices in the industry.

Since then, Mara has dedicated herself exclusively to body jewelry, becoming a strong advocate for safety and quality. She emphasizes the use of implant-grade titanium jewelry and the American needle technique, ensuring a professional, safe, and body-respectful experience.

If you're looking for a piercing done with passion, precision, and the highest quality materials, Mara Corpa is the ideal professional. Visit her and discover how to transform an idea into a work of body art!
