InkYaque, a passionate tattoo artist and expert in color styles

Getting a tattoo is often a pivotal moment in life, and during that special moment, it's essential to have a professional who understands the importance of transforming ideas into permanent art.

My name is Yanire Yaque, although in the tattoo world I’m better known as Inkyaque. My journey began in 2018, when destiny led me to discover my true vocation: tattooing. Since childhood, I’ve always been drawn to art, enjoying drawing, painting, and illustrating. However, it wasn’t until years later, working as a manager in a tattoo studio, that I rediscovered my passion for artistic creation.

Through effort, dedication, and the support of my loved ones, I acquired my first tattoo machine, starting a career that fills me with pride. Today, I have the privilege of tattooing daily in my own studio, La Diva Tattoo, where I specialize in vibrant styles like watercolor, full color, anime, and neo-traditional, always exploring the fascinating world of color.
